# Monitoring questionnaire attribution

The English monitoring prompts are a compact, app-specific question bank based
on the attributed MyChild Engine v0.4.0 / CDC-aligned monitoring material. Any
redistribution of adapted question content must preserve the applicable CC
BY-SA attribution and share-alike terms. Malayalam strings in the app are
machine-translated draft previews only; they are not clinician-reviewed and
cannot generate or submit a live triage result.
